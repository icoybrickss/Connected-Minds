About Us

Elevate your street style with our clothing – where simplicity meets aesthetics in a baller fusion of urban flair.

Embracing vision in every stitch

Connected Minds Clo founded on 2023 started from our passion and love for the culture of the game. Music, Hiphop, Basketball, Street, and Goals are the pillars of what's making our brand stand. Sharing this to the whole community and starting to make a culture of our own.












The Goal

Part of the Community

Uniting in the rhythm of basketball, the essence of local streetwear, and the beat of hip-hop to build a collective identity.

Spread the Culture

Love in music, rhythm in basketball, and style in streetwear – defining our identity in every thread.

Love on the Game

Owners sharing their basketball passion, connecting with people through a love for the game and community.












Our Team

Welcome to the heart and soul of Connected Minds Clo. Our team is not just a collective; we're a family
of diverse individuals brought together by a shared passion for streetwear fashion and
a common goal: to redefine the urban style landscape.












See Us

A humble beginning from The CMNDS Community, started thru meet-ups. Opened a pop-up shop every release.
From 30 people buying to selling out within a week. Who would've thought from making simple designs
that resonates with other people will build a community. Currently we don't have a flagship
store yet, but we are going through it. Stay Connected. You can meet us at
2538 P. Zamora St, Pasay, Metro Manila to view our collections.
